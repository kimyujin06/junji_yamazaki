I am Junji Yamazaki, a freelance designer based in Tokyo, specializing in UI/UX design, web design, and graphic design.

I aim to create designs that not only pursue beauty but also strike a balance between functionality and creativity, ensuring they are easy for users to understand and operate. Additionally, I strive to deeply understand the business goals of each project and deliver designs that exceed client expectations.
